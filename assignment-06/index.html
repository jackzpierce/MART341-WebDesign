Roasted Red Peppers

Ingredients

  • 4 red pepper
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 420oF.
  2. Chop the red pepper into strips.
  3. Spread strips onto the tray evenly.
  4. Coat with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Road the peppers to for 15 minutes,until they have softened.
