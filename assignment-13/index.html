Learn

Learn the history of yoga to bring an informed awareness to the way you teach. Not only will we spend time learning about history and philosophy, but a detailed look at the anatomy will help you keep your students safe.

Practice

Each day will include three (1) hour long sessions. We will build strength and stability through repetition, and deepen our practice each time we come to the mat.

Connect

The rich curriculum and beautiful setting aside, our YTT's draw amazing groups of people from all over the world. The connections you make will last a lifetime, and is one of the most special aspects of completing our trainings.
