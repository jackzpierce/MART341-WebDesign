FAQ

(Frequently Asked Questions)


Q: Do I need a car to attend?
A: No! We provide transportation to and from the airport. (LAX)

Q: Is a YTT expensive?
A: Depending on your income, and YTT can be a significant investment.

Q: What if I can't come up with all the moeny at once?
A: We offer financing plans with low monthly installments!

Q: Will I be certified to teach upon completing the YTT?
A: Yes! As long as your complete the coursework with satisfactory grades.

Q: Will we have an opportunity to teach during the training?
A: Yes! We will start with 10 minute practice classes and finish with full a 60 minutes.

Q: Do I need to be an expert?
A: No! We accept all levels of practicioners.

Q: How long is the training?
A: Two weeks of twelve hour days, with one free day off to relax or explore.

Q: What should I bring?
A: Please bring your own mat, water bottle, sunscreen, and a good book!
