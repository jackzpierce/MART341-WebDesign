Learn

Learn the history of yoga to bring an informed awareness to the way you teach.
Not only will we spend time learning about history and philosophy,

but a detailed look at the anatomy will help you keep your students safe.

Our goal is for you to leave feeling confident and knowledgeable when you walk into a studio to teach.



Practice

Each day will include three (1) hour long sessions.

We will build strength and stability through repetition,
and deepen our practice each time we come to the mat.

Please be ready to work hard both physically and mentally.
We advise increasing your daily practice at home to prepare.




Connect


Our YTT's draw amazing groups of people from all over the world.

The connections you make will last a lifetime, and will be one of the
most profound aspects of completing our trainings.

Come for the education, leave with lifelong friendships.
